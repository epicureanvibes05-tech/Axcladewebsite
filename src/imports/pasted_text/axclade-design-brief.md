# AXCLADE FIGMA-READY DESIGN BRIEF

## Project Name

Axclade Website Landing Page Design

## Brand Name

Axclade

## Brand Slogan

Smart Digital Growth Solutions

## Website Type

Premium digital growth agency landing page

## Primary Goal

Create a modern, premium, conversion-focused landing page that clearly communicates Axclade as an AI-driven digital growth agency helping businesses grow through websites, marketing, branding, automation, apps, software, and scalable digital systems.

---

# 1. Brand Direction

Axclade should feel like a modern digital growth company, not a traditional marketing agency.

The design should communicate:

* Intelligence
* Trust
* Digital growth
* Automation
* Strategy
* Modern technology
* Premium execution
* Business scalability

Overall feeling:

Premium, clean, futuristic, AI-powered, professional, user-friendly, and conversion-focused.

---

# 2. Visual Style Direction

Create a premium, modern, minimal, light-theme landing page.

The website should look like an award-winning Webflow, Framer, SaaS, or AI agency website.

Use:

* Light futuristic background
* Glassmorphism cards
* 3D AI objects
* Dashboard previews
* Floating stat cards
* Soft neon glow
* Red and blue brand accents
* Minimal content
* Strong whitespace
* Modern typography
* Clean section flow
* Subtle visual depth

Avoid:

* Heavy dark background
* Overcrowded content
* Too many colors
* Generic agency layouts
* Flat boring sections
* Identical card designs
* Long paragraphs
* Harsh shadows

---

# 4. Typography Direction

Use a modern geometric sans-serif style similar to Neoverse Sans.

Typography should feel:

* Bold
* Premium
* Clean
* Highly readable
* Modern and digital

## Suggested Desktop Scale

Hero H1:
64px – 76px
Weight: Bold / ExtraBold
Line height: 1.05 – 1.15

Section H2:
36px – 48px
Weight: Bold

Subheadings:
20px – 24px
Weight: SemiBold

Card Titles:
18px – 22px
Weight: SemiBold / Bold

Body Text:
15px – 17px
Weight: Regular
Line height: 1.5 – 1.7

Eyebrow Labels:
11px – 13px
Weight: Bold
Letter spacing: 1px – 2px
Uppercase
Use red or blue accent

---

# 5. Layout System

Use a 12-column desktop grid.

Recommended layout:

* Max container width: 1200px – 1320px
* Section padding: 90px – 130px vertical
* Card radius: 20px – 32px
* Button radius: 999px pill shape
* Card spacing: 20px – 32px
* Large section gap: 100px – 140px

The page must have strong eye movement:

Hero left text → right 3D visual → trust strip → process cards → services grid → why choose section → portfolio → testimonials → CTA → footer.

Every section must feel different from the previous section while staying inside one brand system.
