## Section 13: FAQ Section

### Questions

#### Do you guarantee leads or sales?

No agency can honestly guarantee exact leads or sales because results depend on market, offer, budget, competition, and customer demand. Axclade focuses on improving visibility, tracking, campaigns, conversion performance, and digital systems.

#### Is ad spend included in the package?

No. Ad spend is separate and paid directly by the client to platforms like Meta, Google, LinkedIn, or TikTok.

#### Is there a minimum contract?

Yes. We recommend a minimum 3-month contract because SEO, ads optimization, branding, and digital growth need time to perform properly.

#### Can I start with a small package?

Yes. Starter packages are designed for basic trust-building and setup. Growth packages are recommended for active marketing and lead generation.

#### Do you build custom websites and apps?

Yes. Axclade provides web development, app development, software development, AI automation, and SaaS product development.

#### Which package is best for me?

It depends on your business type, goals, budget, and current digital presence. You can book a free consultation and we will recommend the best option.
